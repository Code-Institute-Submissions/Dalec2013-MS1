Why Travel?

Why do you want to travel? That's the biggest question you have to ask yourself before taking the first step. For me it was, to see the world, meet new people have new scary yet fun experiences. Alot of people want to take there belongings and just go but are scared of the adventure. All i can say to that is if you want it go for it, because if your scared jump otherwise you'll stay where you are forever.
